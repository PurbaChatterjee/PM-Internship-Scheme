PM Internship Scheme

Government of India Initiative

About the Internship

The PM Internship Scheme offers opportunities for students and young professionals to work with government projects and gain real-world experience. For more information, visit:

  • MyGov Official Website
  • PM Internship Portal

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant must be an Indian citizen.
  • Age between 21 - 30 years.
  • Not engaged in full-time employment or education.
  • Enrolled in online/distance learning programmes are eligible.
